Hot Dog Recipe

This is a simple and delicious hot dog recipe that's perfect for a quick lunch or dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 4 hot dog buns
  • 4 hot dogs
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1 cup mustard
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions:

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Place hot dogs on the grill and cook for 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally.
  3. While the hot dogs are cooking, mix together the ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise in a small bowl.
  4. Slice the onion and garlic and sauté in a pan until tender.
  5. Place the hot dogs on the buns and top with the sauce and sautéed vegetables.
